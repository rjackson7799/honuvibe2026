## Description

Welcome to AI Essentials — your starting point for learning AI. In five weeks, you will go from curious to confident. Every class is live, hands-on, and designed so you walk out having done something real with AI. Free ESL English training included for all students.

## Learning Outcomes

- Identify which AI tools matter for your career and skip the ones that don't
- Use AI to complete everyday work tasks: writing, research, translation, and planning
- Ask AI better questions and get dramatically better results
- Use AI to improve meetings, emails, and team communication
- Set up a personal AI assistant configured for your specific job
- Create a clear AI action plan for continued growth after the course

## Target Audience

- Japanese business professionals who want to understand and use AI in their daily work
- Professionals with no prior AI experience looking for a practical, hands-on starting point
- Bilingual professionals who want to leverage AI for cross-cultural communication
- Team leaders exploring how AI can improve their team's productivity and workflows
